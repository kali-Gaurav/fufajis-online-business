# 🎨 UI Themes & Rural Accessibility

The app must be as easy to use as a physical shop counter.

## 1. Multiple Contextual Themes
*   **"Classic Fufaji" (High Contrast)**: Large text, bold buttons, and simple icons for easy use in sunlight.
*   **"Festive Mode" (Diwali/Holi)**: The app UI automatically changes colors (Gold/Red for Diwali, Green for Eid) and adds "Gift Pack" shortcuts.
*   **"Night Mode" (Energy Saving)**: Deep blacks for nighttime operation without eye strain.

## 2. Senior-First Navigation
*   **Zero-Search Interface**: The 10 most common products are always one tap away.
*   **Voice Search (Hindi/Local)**: Customers can say "Dudh aur Bread chahiye" (I want Milk and Bread) to add items to cart.
*   **Image-Heavy Catalog**: For users who may struggle with reading, high-quality images and recognized brand logos take priority over text.

## 3. The "Assistant" UI
*   **Chatbot for Fufaji**: A simple chat bubble where Fufaji can ask: *"Aaj ki kamai kitni hui?"* (How much was today's earning?) and gets a simple reply.
*   **Audio Feedback**: The app speaks back to confirm actions: *"Order pack ho gaya hai"* (Order has been packed).

## 4. Multi-Language (Vernacular)
*   **Dynamic Language Switch**: Toggle between English and Hindi (with local Rajasthani dialects where applicable) instantly across all screens.
*   **Dual-Language Receipts**: WhatsApp receipts printed or sent in both languages for clarity.
