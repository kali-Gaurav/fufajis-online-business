# 🤝 Business Models Built on Trust & Faith

Traditional rural business runs on relationships. This plan digitizes that "Faith" to ensure long-term customer retention.

## 1. Digital Gaon Bahi-Khata (Credit-at-Trust)
*   **The Problem**: Rural customers often pay monthly or after harvests.
*   **The Solution**: A system where Fufaji can assign "Trust Scores" to regular customers.
*   **Functionality**:
    *   One-tap "Add to Udhaar" during checkout.
    *   Automated gentle WhatsApp reminders for balance settlement.
    *   Monthly statement generation in simple local language.

## 2. "Fufaji ki Recommendation" (Curated Picks)
*   **The Concept**: Customers trust Fufaji's word more than an algorithm.
*   **Functionality**:
    *   A special "Fufaji's Top Picks" section on the home screen.
    *   Owner can record a 15-second audio clip explaining why a product is good (e.g., "This batch of Ghee is very pure").
    *   Voice-based reviews from local village influencers.

## 3. Community Group Buying (Samoohik Khareedari)
*   **The Concept**: Lower prices for the whole village if they reach a target quantity.
*   **Functionality**:
    *   "Village Goals": If Bassi village orders 100kg of Sugar together, everyone gets it at ₹5/kg less.
    *   Real-time progress bar visible to all customers in that village.
    *   Encourages neighbors to talk about the app.

## 4. Transparency & Traceability (Sourcing Faith)
*   **The Concept**: Knowing exactly where the food comes from.
*   **Functionality**:
    *   **Farm-to-Phone**: Product pages show the specific farmer's name and village.
    *   **Quality Certificates**: Upload simple photos of purity tests or stock arrivals.

## 5. Subscription for Essentials (Niyamat Aapurti)
*   **The Concept**: Lock in customers for daily needs (Milk, Bread, Eggs).
*   **Functionality**:
    *   "Set it and Forget it" for daily essentials.
    *   Fufaji gets a "Morning Delivery Map" automatically every day at 5 AM.
    *   Auto-billing at the end of the month.
