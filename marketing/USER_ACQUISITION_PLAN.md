# User Acquisition Plan - Fufaji's Online

**Launch Date**: June 22, 2026
**Timeline**: 30-day strategy
**Primary Markets**: Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore (expandable)
**Target**: 1000+ downloads, 500+ active users by end of Month 1

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Fufaji's Online goes to market with a multi-channel acquisition strategy focused on organic growth, word-of-mouth, and strategic paid amplification. The plan emphasizes community engagement and viral mechanics (referral rewards) to achieve sustainable, profitable user growth.

**Month 1 Goal**: 1000 downloads, 500 active users (50% retention)
**Month 2 Goal**: 5000 downloads, 2500 active users
**Month 3 Goal**: 15000 downloads, 7500 active users

---

## 2. Channel Strategy

### 2.1 Organic Channels (Primary)

**Why**: Sustainable, cost-effective, builds community

#### A. Social Media Organic (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook)

**Investment**: $0 (time investment)
**Expected Reach**: 5000-10000 impressions/week
**Conversion Rate**: 2-5%
**Expected Users**: 100-500 from organic

**Tactics**:
1. **Daily Posts**
   - Morning (8 AM): Trending local/grocery topics
   - Afternoon (2 PM): Feature highlights
   - Evening (6 PM): User testimonials
   - Night (8 PM): Engagement posts

2. **Content Mix**
   - 40% Educational (how to use, tips, benefits)
   - 30% Lifestyle (customer stories, shop features)
   - 20% Promotional (offers, rewards, referrals)
   - 10% Behind-the-scenes (team, culture)

3. **Hashtag Strategy**
   - Branded: #FujafiOnline, #FujafiCommunity
   - Generic: #GroceryDelivery, #SupportLocal, #OnlineShopping
   - Location: #DelhiDelivery, #MumbaiGrocery, #BangaloreLocal
   - Trending: Rotate with daily trends

4. **Community Engagement**
   - Respond to all comments within 4 hours
   - Retweet/share user-generated content
   - Run weekly Twitter polls
   - Host monthly Instagram giveaways

**Metrics**: Track reach, engagement rate, clicks to app

---

#### B. WhatsApp Groups & Telegram

**Investment**: $0 (team time)
**Expected Reach**: 2000-5000 direct messages
**Conversion Rate**: 10-20%
**Expected Users**: 200-1000 from direct messaging

**Strategy**:
1. **Create Branded Communities**
   - Fufaji Community Group (WhatsApp)
   - Fufaji Rewards Channel (Telegram)
   - City-specific groups (Delhi Fufaji, Mumbai Fufaji, etc.)

2. **Message Frequency**
   - Daily: 1 main post (morning or lunch time)
   - Weekly: 1 special offer
   - Bi-weekly: 1 product feature or shop spotlight
   - Daily stories/updates (if daily activity)

3. **Content**
   - Order reminders ("Lunch rush? Order in 2 mins!")
   - Shop spotlights ("Meet Fresh Mart - 100+ reviews")
   - Customer wins ("Order delivered in 32 mins!")
   - Exclusive offers ("Group members only!")

4. **Growth Tactics**
   - Encourage members to invite friends
   - Monthly giveaways to active members
   - Exclusive group discounts
   - Shout-out to most loyal customers

**Targets**:
- 5 local WhatsApp groups (500+ members each)
- 1 main Telegram community (200+ members)
- 10 city-specific groups

---

#### C. Word-of-Mouth & Personal Networks

**Investment**: $0
**Expected Reach**: 500-2000 direct referrals
**Conversion Rate**: 30-50% (highest)
**Expected Users**: 150-1000 from personal networks

**Mechanics**:
1. **Internal Referrals**
   - All team members get personal referral codes
   - Each team member challenges to 5-10 referrals
   - Team leaderboard with small prizes

2. **Beta Tester Activation**
   - Thank you email with referral bonus
   - Top 20 beta testers get special recognition
   - Incentivize: ₹50 credit for each referral

3. **Influencer Micro-Seeding**
   - Contact 50 local micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
   - Offer free ₹500 credits
   - Ask for organic posts (not sponsored)
   - Expect 20-30 posts reaching 100K+ total

4. **Community Leaders**
   - Identify community leaders in neighborhoods
   - Partner with them (₹100-200 incentive)
   - They promote to their networks

**Expected Output**:
- Team: 50-100 direct referrals
- Beta testers: 100-200 referrals
- Micro-influencers: 200-500 referrals
- Community leaders: 100-300 referrals
- Total: 450-1100 from networks

**Expected Reach**: 100000-150000
**Conversion Rate**: 2-4%

**Campaign Structure**:

**Creatives** (Test at least 3):
- Video 1: Product browsing → Checkout demo
- Video 2: Order tracking + delivery arrival
- Video 3: Loyalty rewards earning animation
- Static: ₹50 off offer card (high contrast)

**Targeting**:
- Locations: Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore (expandable)
- Age: 18-45
- Interests: Grocery, Food Delivery, Shopping, Local Business
- Lookalike: Similar to email list/beta testers

**Optimization**: Track CPI, pause ads with CPI > ₹50

## 3. Promotional Campaigns & Offers

### 3.1 Launch Week Blitz (Days 1-7)

#### Offer 1: First Order Bonus
- **Promotion**: ₹50 off first order
- **Code**: WELCOME50
- **Validity**: 7 days from app download
- **Mechanics**: Automatic discount at checkout
- **Reach**: All marketing channels
- **Expected Conversions**: 20-30% of downloaders make first order

#### Offer 2: Referral Bonus (Viral)
- **Promotion**: ₹25 for referrer + ₹25 for referred
- **Code**: Personalized (REFER{USERNAME})
- **Validity**: Ongoing
- **Mechanics**: 
  - Referrer gets code to share
  - Referred user enters code at signup
  - Both get ₹25 credit instantly
  - Unlimited referrals
- **Viral Coefficient**: Target 1.5 (each user brings 1.5 new users)
- **Expected Impact**: 30-50% of month 1 growth from referrals

#### Offer 3: Early Bird Bonus (Scarcity)
- **Promotion**: First 500 downloads get extra ₹25
- **Validity**: Days 1-3 only
- **Mechanics**: Automatic bonus at download
- **Purpose**: Create urgency and FOMO
- **Expected Impact**: 500-1000 downloads in first 72 hours

### 3.2 Mid-Month Push (Days 8-15)

#### Offer 4: Loyalty Tier Unlock
- **Promotion**: "Reach Silver tier and unlock 10% off"
- **Target**: Users with 0-2 orders
- **Mechanics**: Gamified progression
- **Purpose**: Increase repeat orders

#### Offer 5: Referral Competition
- **Promotion**: "Top 5 referrers win ₹500 credit"
- **Duration**: 1 week
- **Leaderboard**: Public (gamification)
- **Purpose**: Viral growth spike

### 3.3 Month-End Push (Days 16-30)

#### Offer 6: Bundle Deals
- **Promotion**: "Order 5 times, get ₹200 credit"
- **Target**: Users with 2-4 orders
- **Mechanics**: Auto-credited after 5th order
- **Purpose**: Convert casual users to regular users

#### Offer 7: Shop Spotlight Sales
- **Promotion**: "Partner shop specials - up to 30% off"
- **Rotate**: Different shops each day
- **Mechanics**: Limited inventory
- **Purpose**: Increase order frequency

## 8. Contingency Plans

### If Download Target Missed

**Trigger**: < 200 downloads by Day 4

**Response**:
1. Increase paid ad spend by 50%
2. Launch emergency influencer outreach
3. Double social media frequency
4. Extend launch offer validity
5. Review app store listing (possible UI issue)

---

### If First-Order Rate Low

**Trigger**: < 15% first-order conversion

**Response**:
1. In-app tutorial push
2. Email sequence (onboarding)
3. Increase launch offer discount (₹75 instead of ₹50)
4. Simplify checkout flow
5. A/B test app icon/screenshots

---

### If Crash Rate High

**Trigger**: > 1% crash rate

**Response**:
1. Immediate investigation (Crashlytics)
2. Emergency hotfix deployment
3. Pause paid ads (prevent negative reviews)
4. Pin crash fix post on social media
5. Email affected users with resolution
