## Recommended Architecture

For your Fufaji grocery app:

1. **Full-screen chat module**
2. **Order-specific conversations**
3. **Owner ↔ Employee ↔ Customer communication**
4. **Automatic order notification messages**
5. **Push notifications via FCM**
6. **AI chatbot for common questions**
7. **Ticket assignment system for employees**
8. **Voice notes and image sharing**

This design scales from a small local grocery shop to thousands of customers without changing the architecture.


For your **Fufaji Online Store**, I actually recommend **not using AI at all** in chat. A simple WhatsApp-style communication system will be more reliable and easier for customers.
