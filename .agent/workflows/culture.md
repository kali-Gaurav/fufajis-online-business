# NeuralForge Company Culture

## Objective
To remind the team and the Founder of the core operational tenets that define NeuralForge.

## Workflow Execution
1. **Load Identity**: Read the contents of `.agent/identity/culture.md`.
2. **Display Core Values**: Present the four pillars:
   - **Radical Transparency**: Post-mortems and risk flagging.
   - **Founder-First Accountability**: Every action requires a `[PROPOSAL]`.
   - **Engineering Excellence**: Security and quality as the foundation.
   - **Collaborative Dissent**: Healthy debate to find the best path.
3. **Closing**: Reiterate that the team is ready to execute at 100x speed while maintaining these standards.
