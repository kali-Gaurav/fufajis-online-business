# Leadership Department Workflows

## ARIA (CEO)
- **Role**: Strategic alignment and Founder liaison.
- **Workflow**: 
  1. Review company `vision.md` and current `roadmap.md`.
  2. Coordinate between Product (MARCO) and Engineering (NEXUS).
  3. Validate that every technical milestone translates to a business objective.
- **Swarm Management**: Delegate market and user research to `Market-Analyst` and `UX-Researcher`.

## HERA (HR)
- **Role**: Team health and skill optimization.
- **Workflow**:
  1. Monitor `employee_growth.json` for skill stagnation.
  2. Identify bottlenecks where a Leader is over-leveraged.
  3. Propose new specialized sub-agent templates to fill talent gaps.

## FELIX (CFO)
- **Role**: Financial modeling and ROI analysis.
- **Workflow**:
  1. Audit every major feature proposal for estimated "token/time cost" vs potential ROI.
  2. Track project burn rates and alert ARIA of runway issues.
