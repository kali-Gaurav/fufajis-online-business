# Fufaji's Online: Working System Guide

This guide explains how the system works for both the **Shop Owner (Fufaji)** and the **Customers**, ensuring a seamless real-time experience.

---

## 🏪 For the Shop Owner (Fufaji Side)

We have built a powerful "Business OS" for your shop that makes inventory management effortless.

### 1. Multi-Channel Product Import
You can bring your products online in three ways:
*   **WhatsApp AI Sync**: Simply send a photo of your purchase bill or a handwritten list to our dedicated WhatsApp number. Our AI extracts the product names, prices, and quantities and updates your store instantly.
*   **Bulk CSV Upload**: Use our web/app portal to paste lists from Excel or CSV for massive inventory updates in seconds.
*   **Voice Seeding**: Open the "Voice Add" screen and simply speak: *"Add 20kg Sugar at 45 rupees"*. The system understands and adds it.

### 2. Smart Pricing Engine
Never lose a customer to a competitor. 
*   **Competitor Tracking**: Add prices of nearby stores.
*   **Dynamic Strategies**: Set rules like *"Always beat Competitor A by 2%"* or *"Match the lowest price in the market"*.
*   **Approval Workflow**: The system suggests price changes based on market data, and you just tap "Approve" to update your whole store.

### 3. Real-Time Operations
*   **Packing Terminal**: A dedicated screen for your staff to see new orders, check items off as they pack, and generate OTPs for delivery.
*   **Inventory Health**: Real-time alerts when stock is low or items are near expiry.

---

## 🛍️ For the Customers (User Side)

We have designed the app to feel like a "Personal Assistant" for local shopping.

### 1. Zero-Friction Ordering
*   **Voice Search & Ordering**: Customers can just say what they need. Our bilingual AI (Hindi/English) finds the products.
*   **Quick Reorder**: "Buy Again" section shows what they usually buy, making a weekly grocery run a 2-tap process.
*   **Snap-to-Shop**: Customers can take a photo of their kitchen shelf or a paper list, and the app builds their cart automatically.

### 2. Real-Time Transparency
*   **Live Tracking**: Once an order is "Out for Delivery", the customer sees the rider moving on the map in real-time.
*   **Instant Notifications**: WhatsApp and Push notifications for every stage: Order Confirmed -> Packing -> Out for Delivery -> Delivered.
*   **Flash Deals**: Real-time "Lightning Deals" that expire in an hour, encouraging immediate purchases.

### 3. Hyper-Local Safety
*   **Safe Delivery**: All deliveries are OTP-verified.
*   **Village/District Filter**: Customers see products specifically available or famous in their local village or district first.

## 🛠️ The "Magic" in the Background (Agent Layer)

Our system uses an **Agentic Infrastructure** to keep everything running:
*   **SIGMA (Backend)** handles the real-time data streams and Firestore sync.
*   **NOVA (AI Engineer)** manages the Gemini-powered bill scanning and voice recognition.
*   **NEXUS (CTO)** ensures the architecture scales as you add more products and users.
*   **KYLO (Tech Lead)** maintains the code quality and security of your payments.
