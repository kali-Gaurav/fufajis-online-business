## Getting Started

### Download and Installation

1. **Download the App**
   - Visit Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS)
   - Search for "Fufaji Online"
   - Tap "Install"

2. **Open the App**
   - After installation, launch Fufaji Online
   - You'll see a beautiful welcome screen

### First-Time Setup

#### Step 1: Welcome Screen
- Review the app's key benefits
- Tap "Continue" to proceed

#### Step 2: Set Your Delivery Location
- Enable location permission when prompted
- The app will show nearby shops in your area
- Confirm or manually enter your address
- Select from suggested addresses or add custom address

#### Step 3: Create Your Account
- Enter your phone number
- Receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS
- Enter the OTP to verify
- Add your name and email (optional)
- Tap "Create Account"

#### Step 4: Welcome Discount
- Receive ₹50 off your first order
- Coupon code: **WELCOME50**
- Start shopping!

## Browse & Order

### Explore Products

#### Home Screen
- **Tab 1: Home** - Featured shops and products
- **Tab 2: Search** - Find specific products
- **Tab 3: Orders** - View your order history
- **Tab 4: Wallet** - Manage your credits
- **Tab 5: Account** - Profile and settings

#### Shopping Tips
1. **Search Products**
   - Type product name in search bar
   - Filter by category (Groceries, Fruits, Dairy, etc.)
   - Sort by price, rating, or delivery time

2. **Browse Shops**
   - View shop ratings and delivery time
   - See shop hours and delivery area
   - Check current discounts and offers

3. **View Product Details**
   - Tap any product to see full details
   - Check price, quantity available, and reviews
   - See customer ratings and comments
   - View similar products

### Adding Items to Cart

1. **Select Product**
   - Choose quantity (using +/- buttons)
   - Select variants (size, color, etc.) if available

2. **Add to Cart**
   - Tap "Add to Cart" button
   - Quantity updates in cart icon (top right)

3. **Continue Shopping**
   - Browse more products from same or different shops
   - Each shop's items are grouped in cart

### Cart Management

#### View Your Cart
- Tap cart icon (top right) to review items
- Shows shop name, products, and prices
- Displays delivery time estimate

#### Modify Cart
- Increase/decrease item quantities
- Remove items by tapping trash icon
- Apply coupons or loyalty points

## Checkout

### Review Order

1. **Order Summary**
   - Verify all items and quantities
   - Check delivery address
   - Review pricing breakdown

2. **Delivery Details**
   - Delivery address with map preview
   - Estimated delivery time (usually 30 mins)
   - Change address if needed

### Payment Methods

#### Available Payment Options

1. **Razorpay (Recommended)**
   - Credit/Debit card
   - Net Banking
   - UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
   - Wallet

2. **Fufaji Wallet**
   - Use wallet balance for payment
   - Earn points on every purchase
   - 1 point = ₹1 (redeemable)

3. **Cash on Delivery**
   - Pay when order arrives
   - Only available in select areas

### Apply Coupons

1. **Enter Coupon Code**
   - Tap "Apply Coupon" field
   - Enter code (e.g., WELCOME50)
   - Tap "Apply"

2. **Popular Discount Codes**
   - **WELCOME50** - ₹50 off first order
   - **FIRST25** - ₹25 off orders above ₹200
   - **LOYALTY10** - ₹10 off with loyalty status

3. **View Available Offers**
   - Scroll through "Offers" section
   - Tap to apply automatically

### Place Order

1. **Final Confirmation**
   - Review all details
   - Verify payment method
   - Tap "Place Order"

2. **Order Confirmation**
   - Receive order number (e.g., #FUF-2026-12345)
   - Get SMS/push notification
   - Order appears in "My Orders"

## Track Delivery

### Real-Time Order Tracking

#### Track Your Order
1. Go to **My Orders** tab
2. Tap the current order
3. View live tracking:
   - Order status (preparing, ready, delivered)
   - Rider location on map
   - Estimated arrival time
   - Rider contact info

#### Order Status Flow
```
Confirmed → Preparing → Ready → Picked Up → On the way → Delivered
```

### Contact Rider

1. **Chat with Rider**
   - Tap "Chat" button in order tracking
   - Send messages in real-time
   - Share pickup instructions

2. **Call Rider**
   - Tap phone icon to call directly
   - Share location if needed
   - Request delay/early pickup

### Delivery Issues

**Order Delayed?**
- Check live map for rider location
- Chat with rider or shop
- Don't open the order until it arrives

**Rider Can't Find Address?**
- Share exact location/landmark
- Provide gate number/building name
- Chat with rider for instructions

## After Delivery

### Receive Order

1. **Order Arrival**
   - Rider will call/message
   - Check items before accepting
   - Verify quantity and quality

2. **Payment (if COD)**
   - Pay the exact amount to rider
   - Ask for receipt/confirmation
   - Request digital receipt

### Rate & Review

#### Rate Order

1. **Immediate Rating (Optional)**
   - Rate delivery experience (1-5 stars)
   - Rate rider's behavior
   - Add comments

2. **Rate Products**
   - Go to **My Orders** → Select order
   - Rate each product (1-5 stars)
   - Write reviews about quality, freshness, packaging
   - Upload photos (optional)

#### Benefits of Reviews
- Help other customers decide
- Earn loyalty points (5 points per review)
- Get featured reviewer badge
- Influence shop ratings

### Return or Refund

#### Request Return

1. **Initiate Return**
   - Go to **My Orders**
   - Select order → Tap "Return"
   - Choose reason (wrong item, quality issue, etc.)

2. **Approval Process**
   - Shop owner reviews within 2 hours
   - Approved returns get return label
   - Scan QR code for pickup

3. **Refund Status**
   - Returns processed within 48 hours
   - Refund credited to wallet/original payment
   - Notification sent when complete

#### Return Reasons Accepted
- Product damaged/expired
- Wrong item sent
- Item doesn't match description
- Missing items
- Quality issue

#### Non-Returnable Items
- Fresh produce (except spoiled)
- Consumables (chocolates, candies)
- Frozen items (unless defective)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Payment Failed
**Problem:** Card declined or payment gateway error

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try different payment method
3. Verify card details are correct
4. Wait 5 minutes and retry
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### Order Not Placed
**Problem:** Order wasn't created despite payment

**Solutions:**
1. Check "My Orders" tab first
2. Look for confirmation SMS
3. Try placing order again
4. Contact Fufaji support with error details

#### Wrong Items Delivered
**Problem:** Received different items than ordered

**Solutions:**
1. Don't accept delivery if possible
2. Chat with rider or shop immediately
3. Take photos as evidence
4. Initiate return/exchange
5. Shop will reimburse or replace

#### Rider Can't Find Address
**Problem:** Delivery agent lost or confused

**Solutions:**
1. Enable live location sharing
2. Chat with rider with exact directions
3. Share landmark details (temple, hospital, etc.)
4. Go outside to meet rider
5. Call rider if chat doesn't help

#### Missing Items from Order
**Problem:** Some items missing from delivery

**Solutions:**
1. Check entire package thoroughly
2. Take photos of packaging
3. Contact shop within 2 hours
4. Initiate return/missing item claim
5. Get refund or replacement

#### Can't Login to Account
**Problem:** Forgot password or OTP issue

**Solutions:**
1. Tap "Forgot Password" on login
2. Verify with phone number
3. Enter OTP received via SMS
4. Set new password
5. If no OTP received, contact support

## FAQ

### Ordering Questions

**Q: Do I need an account to order?**
A: Yes, but account creation is quick (2 minutes). Use phone number to sign up.

**Q: How much does delivery cost?**
A: Delivery is ₹20-50 depending on distance. Free for orders > ₹300 with Silver membership.

**Q: Can I schedule orders for later?**
A: Not yet, but coming soon! Currently only immediate delivery is available.

**Q: Are prices the same as in-store?**
A: Usually yes, sometimes better deals online. Check shop prices when browsing.

**Q: What's the minimum order value?**
A: Most shops have ₹100-150 minimum order. Check when selecting shop.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Wallet, and Cash on Delivery.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes! We use Razorpay (PCI-DSS certified). Your data is encrypted.

**Q: Can I get cash back if I overpay?**
A: For UPI/card, no cash back. For COD overpayment, rider will give change.

**Q: Do you accept cryptocurrency or digital wallets?**
A: Currently no. Only standard payment methods.

### Delivery Questions

**Q: How long is delivery?**
A: Usually 20-35 minutes from order placement.

**Q: Can I change delivery address after ordering?**
A: Only if order hasn't been picked up yet. Contact support immediately.

**Q: What if I'm not home when order arrives?**
A: Chat with rider before delivery. Arrange to meet or leave instructions.

**Q: Do you deliver on holidays?**
A: Yes! We deliver 365 days a year. Check individual shop hours.

### Refund Questions

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: 2-5 business days for card/UPI, instant for wallet credit.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, until rider picks up from shop. After that, initiate return.

**Q: What's your return policy?**
A: 48 hours from delivery for quality issues. Read return section above.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I change my phone number?**
A: Go to Account → Settings → Change Phone. Verify with OTP.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, but you can only be logged into one at a time.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Account → Settings → Delete Account. Unverified pending transactions will be cancelled.

**Q: How do I update my address?**
A: Go to Account → Saved Addresses. Add, edit, or delete addresses.

### Loyalty & Rewards Questions

**Q: How long do points stay valid?**
A: Points don't expire as long as you order at least once per year.

**Q: Can I transfer points to others?**
A: No, points are non-transferable and account-specific.

**Q: How do referral rewards work?**
A: Share your link, referred friend orders with ₹50 discount. You get ₹100 wallet credit.

**Q: Can I combine coupons with loyalty points?**
A: No, only one discount method per order. Choose which saves most.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What phones/OS are supported?**
A: iOS 12+ and Android 6.0+

**Q: How much space does the app take?**
A: About 85-95 MB

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: No, you need internet to browse and order.

**Q: Why is the app slow?**
A: Check your internet speed. Try clearing app cache (Account → Settings).
