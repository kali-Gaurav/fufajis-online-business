# Fufaji Online - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Customer FAQ

### Ordering & Shopping

**Q1: How do I place my first order on Fufaji?**
A: Download the app → Sign up with phone number → Verify with OTP → Set delivery address → Browse products → Add to cart → Checkout with coupon code WELCOME50 for ₹50 off.

**Q2: What products can I order?**
A: We deliver groceries, fruits, vegetables, dairy, bakery items, and more from local shops. Browse the app to see what's available in your area.

**Q3: Are prices the same as physical stores?**
A: Mostly yes! Some items might be cheaper online due to promotional offers. Check the app for current pricing.

**Q4: Can I schedule orders for a specific time?**
A: Currently, we only offer immediate delivery (20-40 minutes). Scheduled delivery coming soon.

**Q5: What's the minimum order amount?**
A: Most shops have ₹100-150 minimum order. Some have no minimum. Check when selecting a shop.

**Q6: Can I order from multiple shops?**
A: You can add items from one shop per order. For multiple shops, place separate orders.

**Q7: Do you deliver on Sundays/Holidays?**
A: Yes! We deliver 365 days a year. Check individual shop hours (some may close on specific holidays).

**Q8: How do I cancel an order?**
A: Go to "My Orders" → Tap the order → "Cancel Order" (only before rider picks up from shop).

---

### Delivery & Tracking

**Q9: How long is typical delivery time?**
A: Most orders arrive within 20-40 minutes from placement. Premium express service delivers in 15 minutes (higher fee).

**Q10: How can I track my delivery?**
A: Go to "My Orders" → Select active order → View live GPS tracking, rider location, and ETA.

**Q11: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?**
A: Only if the order hasn't been picked up by the rider. Contact support immediately if needed.

**Q12: What if I'm not home when the order arrives?**
A: Chat with the rider before delivery to arrange an alternative time or leave instructions (e.g., "Leave at gate").

**Q13: Can I talk to the rider?**
A: Yes! Every order has a chat option. You can also call the rider directly through the app.

**Q14: What if the order is late?**
A: Track the rider's location. If delayed beyond 15 mins from ETA, chat with the rider or contact support for a refund/discount.

---

### Payment & Pricing

**Q15: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit/Debit cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, etc.), Net Banking, Razorpay Wallet, Fufaji Wallet, and Cash on Delivery (select areas).

**Q16: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Absolutely! We use Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified) for all payments. Your data is encrypted.

**Q17: Why was my payment declined?**
A: Check: (1) Sufficient card balance, (2) Card isn't expired, (3) No daily transaction limit exceeded, (4) Card isn't blocked by your bank. Retry or use different payment method.

**Q18: Can I get a refund if I cancel the order?**
A: Yes! Refunds are instant for digital payments (wallet credit) and 2-5 business days for cards/UPI.

**Q19: What if I was charged twice?**
A: This rarely happens due to payment gateway protections. Contact support with transaction ID, and we'll investigate within 24 hours.

**Q20: Do you accept cryptocurrency?**
A: Currently no. Only standard payment methods accepted.

---

### Returns & Refunds

**Q21: Can I return items after delivery?**
A: Yes, within 48 hours for quality issues (damaged, expired, wrong item). Fresh produce can't be returned once delivered.

**Q22: How do I initiate a return?**
A: Go to "My Orders" → Select order → "Return Order" → Choose reason → Submit. Shop approves within 2 hours.

**Q23: What's refundable?**
A: Quality issues (damage, expiry, wrong item). Non-refundable: Fresh produce (except spoiled), consumables, or orders past 48-hour window.

**Q24: How long do refunds take?**
A: Instantly to Fufaji Wallet. 2-5 business days for original payment method (card/UPI).

**Q25: Can I exchange items instead of returning?**
A: Yes, the shop will offer exchange option if similar item is available. Otherwise, full refund.

### Loyalty & Rewards

**Q26: How do loyalty points work?**
A: Earn 1 point per ₹1 spent. Redeem 1 point = ₹1 credit on future orders.

**Q27: Do points expire?**
A: No, points don't expire as long as you order once per year.

**Q28: What are loyalty tiers?**
A: Bronze (0-500), Silver (501-2000), Gold (2001+). Higher tiers give better discounts (0% to 10% off).

**Q29: How do I level up tiers?**
A: Earn points through purchases, reviews, and referrals. Tiers are cumulative lifetime points.

**Q30: Can I transfer points to another person?**
A: No, points are personal and non-transferable.

**Q31: How does the referral program work?**
A: Share your code with friends. Each successful order (₹200+) from referred friend gives you ₹100, friend gets ₹50 off.

**Q32: How many people can I refer?**
A: Unlimited! No cap on referral earnings.

### Technical Issues

**Q33: The app keeps crashing. What should I do?**
A: (1) Force close app, (2) Clear cache (Settings → Apps → Fufaji → Storage), (3) Restart phone, (4) Reinstall if issue persists.

**Q34: Why is the app so slow?**
A: Check internet speed (2G won't work). Close background apps. Clear cache. Update to latest version.

**Q35: Why can't I see products in my area?**
A: Either: (1) No shops deliver to your location, (2) All shops are closed, (3) Location permission not enabled. Check settings.

**Q36: How do I update the app?**
A: Go to Play Store/App Store → Fufaji Online → Update button. Auto-update can be enabled in app settings.

**Q37: Does Fufaji work without internet?**
A: No, you need active internet (WiFi or mobile data) to browse and order.

**Q38: Why is my location showing wrong?**
A: Go to Settings → Location services → Disable and re-enable → Restart app. Or manually set address.

### Account & Security

**Q39: How do I change my phone number?**
A: Account → Settings → Change Phone Number → Verify with OTP → Confirm. You'll need to re-verify for orders.

**Q40: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, but you can only login to one at a time per device.

**Q41: How do I delete my account?**
A: Account → Settings → Delete Account → Confirm. All personal data and orders will be permanently deleted.

**Q42: What data does Fufaji collect?**
A: Name, phone, email, delivery address, order history, payment info (not full details), device ID, location. We never sell data to third parties.

**Q43: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! We use 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Compliant with GDPR and Indian data protection laws.

**Q44: How do I report a security issue?**
A: Email security@fufaji.com with detailed information. We reward responsible disclosure.

## Shop Owner FAQ

### Setup & Registration

**Q45: How do I register my shop?**
A: Download app → Select "Shop Owner" → Add shop details → Verify phone → Add address → Set hours → Add products → Go live!

**Q46: What documents do I need to register?**
A: Government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, DL), Shop license/registration, Bank account details.

**Q47: How long does verification take?**
A: 24-48 hours. You'll get notification when approved.

**Q48: Can I manage multiple shops?**
A: Yes! Register each shop separately with different phone numbers.

**Q49: What are the shop registration fees?**
A: Free to register. We charge 8-12% commission on successful orders.

---

### Product Management

**Q50: How do I add products to my shop?**
A: Go to "Inventory" → "Add Product" → Fill name, price, category, images, quantity → Save.

**Q51: What's the maximum number of products?**
A: No limit! Add as many as you want.

**Q52: Can I bulk upload products?**
A: Yes! Go to "Inventory" → "Bulk Upload" → Download CSV template → Fill and upload.

**Q53: How do I set product expiry dates?**
A: When adding product, fill "Expiry Date" field. App will auto-hide expired products from customers.

**Q54: Can I create product bundles/combos?**
A: Yes! Go to "Offers" → "Create Bundle" → Select products → Set combo price → Activate.

**Q55: How do I manage inventory/stock?**
A: Products show current stock. When stock runs low, system alerts you. Set minimum stock level in settings.

**Q56: Can I offer coupons/discounts?**
A: Yes! Go to "Offers" → "Create Coupon" → Set code, discount, validity → Activate.

---

### Order Management

**Q57: How do I receive orders?**
A: Orders appear in "Pending Orders" section. You have 5 minutes to accept/reject.

**Q58: What happens if I reject an order?**
A: Customer gets auto-refunded. Order cancellation reason is logged.

**Q59: How do I manage order fulfillment?**
A: Once accepted: (1) Confirm order, (2) Start packing, (3) Mark as packed, (4) Assign rider pickup.

**Q60: Can I assign multiple riders to one order?**
A: No, each order has one rider. But multi-item orders from different shops go to different riders.

**Q61: Can I update order status manually?**
A: Yes, but system auto-updates based on rider and customer actions. Manual updates only for corrections.

---

### Payments & Settlements

**Q62: When do I get paid?**
A: Daily settlements! Money transferred to your bank account every night (T+1).

**Q63: How much do I earn per order?**
A: Order total minus Fufaji commission (8-12%) minus taxes. Shown in real-time dashboard.

**Q64: What's the minimum withdrawal amount?**
A: ₹100. No maximum limit.

**Q65: Can I change my bank account?**
A: Yes! Go to "Settings" → "Bank Account" → "Update" → Add new account details.

**Q66: Are there any transaction fees?**
A: No! Fufaji commission is the only deduction.

**Q67: What if a payment fails?**
A: Failed orders are still recorded. You can retry settlement or contact support.

---

### Operations & Logistics

**Q68: How do I manage delivery area?**
A: Settings → Delivery Area → Draw on map or enter pin codes. Customers outside won't see your shop.

**Q69: What if I run out of stock mid-day?**
A: Products with 0 stock auto-hide from customer view. No new orders accepted for those items.

**Q70: Can I set different prices for different areas?**
A: Currently no, but coming soon! Prices are shop-wide for now.

**Q71: How do riders pick up orders?**
A: Fufaji assigns rider → You pack items → Rider arrives → Scans QR code → Picks up → Delivers.

**Q72: What if a rider doesn't show up?**
A: Contact support immediately with order details. We'll reassign within 15 minutes.

**Q73: Can I hire my own delivery staff?**
A: Not directly through app, but you can coordinate separately. Fufaji handles logistics.

### Support & Troubleshooting

**Q74: How do I contact support?**
A: Go to "Help" → "Contact Support" → Chat with agent (average response: 10 minutes).

**Q75: How do I handle customer complaints?**
A: Use in-app support system. Respond to complaints within 2 hours for quick resolution.

**Q76: What if a customer reports missing items?**
A: Investigate and provide refund/replacement. Habitual errors harm your rating.

**Q77: How do refunds to customers work?**
A: Fufaji refunds customer from your balance. Settlement shows as deduction.

**Q78: Can I disable my shop temporarily?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Shop Status → "On Break". Reactivate anytime.

## Rider FAQ

### Registration & Verification

**Q79: How do I register as a rider?**
A: Download app → Select "Rider" → Fill personal details → Upload documents → Wait for approval (24-48 hours).

**Q80: What documents do I need?**
A: Government ID (Aadhaar/DL/Passport), Vehicle registration, Insurance, Driving license, Profile photo.

**Q81: What vehicles are eligible?**
A: Bikes, scooters, autos, cars, bicycles. Must have valid registration and insurance.

**Q82: How much do I earn?**
A: Base pay per delivery (₹30-50) + Tips from customers. Bonus for on-time deliveries.

**Q83: Do I need my own vehicle?**
A: Yes, you need to own or lease a vehicle. Fufaji doesn't provide vehicles.

---

### Earning & Payouts

**Q84: How often do I get paid?**
A: Daily! Earnings credited to your account every night (T+1).

**Q85: What's the minimum earnings to withdraw?**
A: ₹100. No maximum limit.

**Q86: Are there deductions from my earnings?**
A: Only if you cancel accepted orders (₹50 penalty). Everything else is yours.

**Q87: How do tips work?**
A: Customers can tip after delivery. Tips go 100% to you.

**Q88: Can I see expected earnings before accepting order?**
A: Yes! Each order shows base pay and estimated delivery time.

### Deliveries & Operations

**Q89: How do I accept orders?**
A: Enable "Accept Orders" → Orders appear in queue → Tap to accept → Navigate to shop → Pick up → Deliver.

**Q90: What if I can't accept an order?**
A: Just don't tap it. After 30 seconds, system assigns to another rider. No penalty.

**Q91: How do I navigate to shop/customer?**
A: App integrates with Google Maps. Tap "Navigate" for turn-by-turn directions.

**Q92: What if I need to cancel an accepted order?**
A: Tap "Cancel" → Explain reason → Submit. First 3 cancellations/month free, then ₹50 penalty each.

**Q93: How do I get a signature/proof of delivery?**
A: App captures photo and signature (or digital signature) before marking delivered.

**Q94: Can I reject cash payments?**
A: No, you must accept cash or card when customer uses COD payment.

**Q95: What if customer disputes delivery?**
A: Your photo/signature proof is used. Support team reviews within 2 hours.

### Ratings & Performance

**Q96: How are riders rated?**
A: Customers rate 1-5 stars after delivery. Ratings based on timeliness, behavior, condition of order.

**Q97: What's the minimum acceptable rating?**
A: 4.0+ is good. Below 4.0, you get additional training. Below 3.5, deactivated until improvement.

**Q98: How do I improve my rating?**
A: Deliver on time, be courteous, protect order condition, communicate well with customer.

**Q99: Can I see individual ratings/feedback?**
A: Yes! Go to "My Profile" → "Ratings" → View detailed feedback.

**Q100: Are perfect ratings rewarded?**
A: Yes! 4.8+: ₹500/month bonus. 4.9+: ₹1000/month bonus. 5.0: ₹2000/month bonus.
